# 🚀 Project Title
## FinWise AI — Smart Financial Guidance for a Financially Empowered ASEAN

FinWise AI is an **AI-powered personal financial assistant** designed to help users **track expenses, analyze financial habits, and receive intelligent financial advice**.

The platform focuses on **financial literacy, budgeting discipline, and financial awareness**, particularly for **students and young professionals across Southeast Asia.**

FinWise AI promotes **smarter financial behavior and long-term financial stability** through accessible artificial intelligence.

# 🌏 Vision

FinWise AI aims to become a **digital financial companion for ASEAN users**.

The platform helps individuals:

• Understand spending habits  
• Build stronger saving behavior  
• Improve financial decision-making  
• Achieve long-term financial stability

---

# ⚙️ How FinWise AI Works

## 1️⃣ Expense Tracking

Users record daily expenses in categories such as:

• 🍜 Food  
• 🚗 Transport  
• 🛍 Shopping  
• 💡 Bills  

The system organizes these records to help users **visualize spending behavior.**

---

## 2️⃣ Financial Analysis

FinWise AI processes expense data and generates insights including:

• Spending distribution  
• Monthly financial trends  
• Budget discipline indicators  
• Savings potential  

This allows users to **better understand their financial habits.**

---

## 3️⃣ AI Financial Assistant

Users interact with an **AI-powered chatbot** that provides personalized financial guidance.

Example questions:

```
Can I afford to buy a RM500 phone?
How can I save more money next month?
Is my spending too high this week?
```

The AI analyzes financial records and produces **context-aware financial recommendations.**

---

## 4️⃣ Financial Health Score

FinWise AI calculates a **Financial Health Score** based on several indicators:

• Spending Control  
• Savings Rate  
• Budget Discipline  
• Emergency Fund Readiness  
• Financial Consistency  

This score helps users monitor their **overall financial wellness.**

---

## 5️⃣ Savings Goals

Users can set financial goals such as:

• 💻 Buying a laptop  
• ✈️ Travel savings  
• 🛡 Emergency fund  

The system tracks progress and suggests **monthly savings targets.**
